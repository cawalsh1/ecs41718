ABOUT


Born and raised in sunny California, I've always had a connection to the ocean. In fact, ever since I was a child I knew I wanted to grow up and become a marine biologist. When I moved to Chicago at age 7, I really started to take action towards that goal. I got my scuba certification at age 12, the same year I started going to marine biology camp in the Florida Keys. This year, I'm applying for an internship at the Shedd Aquarium. Besides the ocean, I lvoe playing my electric guitar whenever I can, which can sometimes interfere with homework.
